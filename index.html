About the Conference

This proposal outlines the plan to jointly organize an International Workshop followed by an International Conference through a collaborative partnership between Dr. Ashok Gujar Technical Institute’s , Dr. Daulatrao Aher College of Engineering Karad,India and CTIF Global Capsule (CGC), Denmark. The initiative aims to promote high‐quality academic exchange, innovation-driven research, and global collaboration in emerging areas of science, engineering, technology, and sustainable development

OBJECTIVE

1. To provide a global platform for researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students to share knowledge, research outcomes, 
   and best practices.
2. To strengthen academic and research collaboration between CU Mizoram and CGC Denmark.
3. To promote interdisciplinary research in areas aligned with global priorities such as Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), 
   Digital Transformation, Smart Technologies, AI, IoT, and Sustainable Innovation.
4. To identify future joint projects, publications, and capacity-building initiatives.
5. To enhance international visibility and academic reputation of both institutions.
